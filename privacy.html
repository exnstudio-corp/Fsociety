Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025 · Operated by EXN Studio

Overview

F-Society ("the Platform") is operated by EXN Studio. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and what rights you have regarding your data. By using F-Society, you consent to the practices described in this document.

We are committed to minimizing data collection and never selling your information to third parties.

Information We Collect

We collect only what is necessary to operate the platform:

  • Username — chosen by you during registration. Used to identify you within the platform and in the chat system.
  • Authentication data — a hashed password managed securely by Supabase Authentication. We never store your plaintext password.
  • Account metadata — your account creation timestamp and a derived member ID. Not linked to any external identity.
  • Chat messages — text messages you post in the global chat are stored in our database and visible to all logged-in members.
  • Uploaded text files — .txt files you voluntarily upload to the chat are stored in Supabase Storage and available for download by other members.
  • Usage timing data — approximate timestamps of actions (message sent, file uploaded) for moderation and rate-limiting purposes.

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Real name, government-issued ID, or national identification
  • Banking information, payment details, or financial records
  • Phone number or physical address
  • Email address (our system uses an internal identifier, not your real email)
  • Browser fingerprint or device hardware identifiers
  • Location data beyond what your browser may expose in requests
  • Behavioral tracking or advertising profiles

We do not use analytics platforms (e.g., Google Analytics), advertising networks, or third-party tracking pixels.

How We Use Your Information

  • To create and authenticate your account
  • To display your username in the chat and throughout the platform
  • To enforce daily upload limits and registration rate limits
  • To store and serve chat messages and uploaded files to logged-in members
  • To schedule and process account deletions when requested
  • To maintain platform security and prevent abuse

We do not use your data for marketing, profiling, or any purpose beyond operating the platform.

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored using Supabase, a secure backend-as-a-service platform. Supabase applies row-level security (RLS) policies to control access. Passwords are hashed using industry-standard algorithms and are never stored in plaintext.

The platform is hosted on Netlify. Environment variables and API keys are stored in Netlify's encrypted environment variable system and are never exposed in client-side code.

Despite these measures, no system is 100% secure. You are responsible for keeping your account credentials safe and for the content you choose to share.

User-Generated Content

Content you post — chat messages and uploaded .txt files — is visible to all registered members of F-Society. You are solely responsible for the content you share. Do not post content that violates our Terms & Conditions or applicable law.

EXN Studio may remove content at any time if it violates community standards, without prior notice.

Local Storage

F-Society uses your browser's localStorage to store:

  • Splash screen acknowledgement (to avoid showing it on every visit)
  • Registration rate-limit state (to enforce the 24-hour window client-side)
  • Daily file upload count (to enforce the 3-files-per-day limit client-side)
  • Your username (for display purposes after login)

This data is stored only in your browser and is not transmitted to our servers. Clearing your browser data will reset these states.

Data Retention

  • Chat messages are retained indefinitely unless removed by moderation.
  • Uploaded files are retained until manually removed or upon account deletion.
  • Account data is retained until deletion is confirmed. After a 14-day grace period following a deletion request, your account and associated data are permanently removed.

Third-Party Services

F-Society uses the following third-party services. Their respective privacy policies govern the data they process:

  • Supabase — database, authentication, and file storage (supabase.com/privacy)
  • Netlify — hosting and edge functions (netlify.com/privacy)
  • JSONBin.io — educational article storage (jsonbin.io/privacy-policy)
  • Ionicons (unpkg CDN) — icon assets
  • Google Fonts — typography

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request deletion of your account and associated data at any time through the Account section
  • Know what data is stored about you (limited to username, join date, member ID, messages, and files)
  • Refrain from sharing any information beyond what registration requires

For data-related requests not covered by the in-app tools, contact EXN Studio through the official website: exn-fsociety.netlify.app.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the platform after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

Contact

Questions about this Privacy Policy can be directed to EXN Studio at: exn-fsociety.netlify.app
